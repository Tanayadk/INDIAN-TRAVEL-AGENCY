Konkan

Serene Coastline of Maharashtra

About Konkan

Konkan is a beautiful coastal region of Maharashtra known for its pristine beaches, lush greenery, coconut trees, and traditional culture. It offers a peaceful escape from city life.

Top Places to Visit

Ganpatipule

Famous beach with Ganpati temple.

Tarkarli

Crystal clear water & scuba diving.

Ratnagiri

Known for Alphonso mangoes & forts.

Things to Do

  • 🏖 Relax on untouched beaches
  • 🤿 Try scuba diving in Tarkarli
  • 🍤 Enjoy authentic seafood
  • 🌴 Explore coconut plantations

Travel Info

Best Time: October to March

How to Reach: Road, Konkan Railway
