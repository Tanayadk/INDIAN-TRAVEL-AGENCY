About Lonavala

Lonavala is a famous hill station in Maharashtra known for its green valleys, waterfalls, and cool climate. It is a perfect getaway during monsoon and weekends.

Top Places to Visit

Lion's Point

Best spot for sunrise and valley view.

Bhushi Dam

Popular monsoon destination.

Karla Caves

Ancient Buddhist rock-cut caves.

Things to Do

  • 🌄 Trekking & hiking
  • 🌧 Enjoy monsoon waterfalls
  • 📸 Photography at viewpoints
  • 🍬 Try famous Lonavala chikki

Travel Info

Best Time: June to September (monsoon)

How to Reach: Road & railway from Mumbai/Pune
