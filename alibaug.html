Alibaug

Beach Paradise Near Mumbai

About Alibaug

Alibaug is a popular coastal destination known for its beaches, forts, and relaxing atmosphere. It is a perfect weekend getaway from Mumbai and Pune.

Top Places to Visit

Alibaug Beach

Famous for sunset views.

Kolaba Fort

Historic fort in the sea.

Kihim Beach

Peaceful and clean beach.

Things to Do

  • 🏖 Enjoy beach sunsets
  • 🚤 Water sports
  • 🍤 Try seafood
  • 🏰 Explore forts

Travel Info

Best Time: Nov - Feb

How to Reach: Ferry or road
