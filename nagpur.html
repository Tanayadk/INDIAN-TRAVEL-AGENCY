About Nagpur

Nagpur is known as the geographical center of India and is famous for its oranges. It is a growing city with cultural heritage, wildlife nearby, and modern infrastructure.

Top Places to Visit

Important Buddhist monument.

Beautiful lake and garden.

Wildlife safari destination.
