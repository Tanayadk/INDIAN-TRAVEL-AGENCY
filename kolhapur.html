Kolhapur

City of Temples & Famous Kolhapuri Cuisine

About Kolhapur

Kolhapur is a culturally rich city known for its historic temples, traditional lifestyle, and world-famous spicy Kolhapuri food. It is also known for handicrafts and royal heritage.

Top Places to Visit

Mahalaxmi Temple

One of the most important Shakti Peethas.

Radhanagari Dam

Beautiful scenic spot surrounded by nature.

Panhala Fort

Historic fort with great views and history.

Things to Do

  • 🍛 Try authentic Kolhapuri cuisine
  • 🏛 Visit temples and heritage sites
  • 🛍 Shop Kolhapuri chappals
  • 📸 Explore forts and nature spots

Travel Info

Best Time: October - February

How to Reach: By road, rail, and airport nearby

Famous Food: Kolhapuri Thali, Tambda Rassa, Pandhra Rassa
