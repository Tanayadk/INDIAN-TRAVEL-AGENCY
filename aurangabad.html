Aurangabad

Gateway to Ajanta & Ellora Caves

About Aurangabad

Aurangabad is a historic city known for its rich Mughal heritage and world-famous UNESCO sites like Ajanta and Ellora caves. It is one of the most culturally significant cities in Maharashtra.

Top Places to Visit

Ajanta Caves

Ancient Buddhist caves with paintings.

Ellora Caves

Rock-cut temples including Kailasa temple.

Bibi Ka Maqbara

Mini Taj Mahal of the Deccan.

Things to Do

  • 🏛 Explore historical monuments
  • 📸 Photography at heritage sites
  • 🕌 Visit Mughal architecture
  • 🍛 Try local Mughlai cuisine

Travel Info

Best Time: October - March

How to Reach: By air, train, or road

Famous Food: Naan Qalia, Mughlai dishes
